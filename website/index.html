Welcome to Sasko's Kitchen

Here at Sasko's Kitchen Fine dining made personal. Whether you’re looking for
a reliable private chef for an intimate evening, a welcoming restaurant experience,
or catering for a special occasion, Saskos Kitchen has you covered.

We specialize in creating unforgettable dining experiencesfrom private functions,
birthdays, and corporate events to weddings and family gatherings. Our food truck
also brings Saskos Kitchen flavors to local events, festivals, and community
celebrations, serving up the same love, quality, and attention to detail you’d
find in our restaurant.

At Saskos Kitchen, we believe in combining premium ingredients, sustainable practices,
and genuine hospitality to deliver meals that feel as good as they taste.

For bookings, reservations, or event inquiries, give us a call or connect with us
through our social media pages we’d love to bring our kitchen to your table.


Saskos Kitchen's committed

Saskos Kitchen is committed to sourcing local, sustainable, and organic ingredients
whenever possible. We prepare everything from scratch with care and passion, using
only premium ingredients.

We believe in treating people right our employees earn living wages, and tips are
shared with the entire team, including kitchen staff. We also strive to minimize our
environmental footprint in everything we do.

These commitments may mean slightly higher menu prices, but we hope you see the value
in knowing that every meal supports fair wages, sustainability, and food made with love
Every day, we’re working to get better at what matters most.
